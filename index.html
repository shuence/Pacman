The development of this Pac-Man game is my attempt to learn and explore HTML5 games, and it is also a tribute to this classic arcade game. The game roughly restores the appearance of Pac-Man in my impression. While transplanting levels and rules of gameplay, the game character animation management and ghost intelligent addressing algorithms are added to the game, which realizes the enclosure of the player by ghosts. I hope to share some of my thoughts on game development with you through the game and code.
